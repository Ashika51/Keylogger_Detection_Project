Support & Keylogger Awareness

Keyloggers are malicious programs that secretly record your keyboard activity. They can steal your passwords, banking information, and personal messages. To protect yourself:

  • Regularly scan your system with anti-malware tools.
  • Check your startup programs for unknown applications.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA wherever possible.
  • Do not download software from untrusted sources.
  • Keep your operating system and antivirus up-to-date.
